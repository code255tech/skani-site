Frequently Asked Questions

What is the school starting age?

We admit children into Kindergarten from age 3. Primary starts at age 6.

Is there a school uniform?

Yes, the school uniform is mandatory. It can be purchased at the school admin office.

How can I pay school fees?

We accept bank transfers, NMB Wakala, or cheque. Cash is not accepted at the school office for security reasons.

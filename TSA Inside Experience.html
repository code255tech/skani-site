Apartment Guidance

Air Conditioning

  • Use remote to turn ON/OFF
  • Recommended temp: 22–24°C
  • Keep doors/windows closed for efficiency

Smart TV

  • Use TV remote for Netflix / YouTube
  • Ensure TV is connected to TSA WiFi
  • Please log out of personal accounts before check-out

Kitchen & Appliances

  • Stove: Push knob in & turn to ignite
  • Kettle: Ensure base is plugged in & switch ON
  • Utensils & cookware are in the kitchen drawers

Laundry

  • Washing machine is inside the laundry cabinet
  • Add detergent → select mode → press start
  • A drying rack is provided in the closet

Hot Water

Hot water is available 24/7. Please allow a few seconds for it to reach the tap after turning on.

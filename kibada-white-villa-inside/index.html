Villa Guide

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in time is at 2:00 PM. Check-out time is strictly at 11:00 AM to allow cleaning for succeeding guests. Late checkout triggers surcharges.

Pool Safety Reminders

Strictly no diving is allowed as the pool is shallow. Never bring glass containers or cups inside or immediately surrounding the pool deck. Supervise kids at all times.

BBQ Usage Guide

Barbecue facilities are open for guest enjoyment. Ensure you clean the grills post-cooking. Double check that all gas valves are shut tight after finishing.

Smoking Areas

Strictly no smoking inside the villa or bedrooms. Designated smoking zones are located strictly on the outdoor open terraces and garden grounds.

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are strictly observed from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM out of respect for neighboring properties. Avoid loud speaker setups during these hours.

Property Care Guidelines

Please respect our items and custom fixtures. Avoid dragging pool cues, sit or sit on edge cushions, and secure doors while air conditioning is fully active.

Emergency Contacts

For fast host emergency assistance, trigger our emergency hotlines or call the host at 0675029099 instantly.

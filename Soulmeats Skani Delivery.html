Get the Best Results

1

Place where customers pause

Good spots: tables, entrance, cashier, waiting area. → This increases scans immediately.

2

Make it easy to see and scan

Avoid: dark areas, glare (glass reflection), very small prints. → If it’s hard to scan, people won’t try again.

3

Add a simple instruction

Example: “Scan to view menu” or “Scan to explore”. → People are more likely to scan when told what to expect.

4

Test before printing in bulk

Scan using different phones (Android & iPhone). → Ensures everything works perfectly before spending money.

5

Use your link online too

Add to Instagram bio, WhatsApp, Google Business. → More traffic beyond just physical scans.

6

Keep your Skani updated

Update offers, content, or info regularly. → Fresh content keeps customers engaged.

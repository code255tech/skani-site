Room Guide

🍽️ Kitchen

Plates, cups, and glasses are inside the upper cupboards above the sink. The gas/stove is ready to use (turn knob to ignite). Please wash dishes after use.

🔥 Microwave

Place food inside a microwave-safe container. Set time using dial/buttons and press start. Wait before opening (hot steam warning).

🧺 Laundry

Washing machine located in laundry area. Load clothes (do not overfill), add detergent in slot, select quick wash mode, and press start.

💡 Electricity & AC

Light switches are near the entrance. The AC remote is in the room. Please turn off both when leaving the room.

🚿 Bathroom

Hot water is available (turn the heater switch on if needed). Please conserve water.
